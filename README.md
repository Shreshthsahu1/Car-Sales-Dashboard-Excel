# 🚗 Car Sales Analytics Dashboard (Excel)

![Dashboard](Dashboard.png)

## 📌 Project Overview

Developed an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze over **1 million synthetic car sales records**. The dashboard provides business insights into sales performance, customer preferences, vehicle conditions, pricing trends, and regional distribution using Excel's data analysis capabilities.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze car sales across different countries.
- Compare sales performance over multiple years.
- Identify popular car brands and colors.
- Evaluate vehicle conditions.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- 💰 Total Sales
- 🌍 Sales by Country
- 🚘 Average Car Price
- 📅 Sales by Year
- 🎨 Sales by Car Color
- 🚗 Vehicle Condition Analysis
